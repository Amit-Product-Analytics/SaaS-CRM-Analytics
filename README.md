# SaaS CRM Analytics Dashboard

## Project Overview

This project analyzes a SaaS CRM business using SQL, Power BI, Excel, and DAX.

The objective of this project is to understand recurring revenue performance, customer growth, churn behavior, retention trends, product engagement, and overall business health.

---

## Business Problem

A SaaS company needs to answer key business questions:

* How much MRR and ARR is generated?
* What is the customer churn rate?
* How many trial users convert into paying customers?
* Which subscription plans generate the highest revenue?
* How much revenue is lost due to churn?
* Which customer segments contribute the most revenue?
* How engaged are users with the product?

---

## Key Insights

* Enterprise customers generated the highest revenue contribution.
* Trial conversion rate reached 66.6%.
* Churn rate remained close to 20%.
* Customer upgrades significantly increased recurring revenue.
* Higher product engagement resulted in stronger retention.
